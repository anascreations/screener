💡Why "Morning Scan, Afternoon Buy"?
Anti Bull Trap

Mornings often see false breakouts (fakeouts). Afternoon prices are "honest" prices agreed upon by institutions before market close.

Candle Confirmation

Buying at 4:30 PM (MY) ensures the daily candle shape is finalized. If it remains strong green, momentum is likely to continue tomorrow.

Volume Reality

Low Volume Ratio in the morning is normal. By afternoon, it reveals the true "winner" of the day's market battle.

🛡Risk Management Rules (ATR-Based)
Use ATR14 data from Moomoo in TradeMatrix for accurate calculations. Set an automatic Stop Loss in Moomoo immediately after buying.
